# Business Brief Template: [Course Title]

## 1. Business Context & Problem Statement
*Describe the organizational background, the specific problem being addressed, and why this training is critical at this moment. Detail the operational urgency and the impact of the current skill gap.*

**Drafting Guidance:**
- Avoid generic descriptions like "improve productivity."
- Specify the business unit (e.g., "North American Sales Operations").
- Quantify the pain if possible (e.g., "30% increase in error rates since system migration").

## Authoring Quality Checklist
- [ ] Business context is deep enough for a Strategy Lead to start with ZERO clarification.
- [ ] Goals are specific, measurable, and business-focused.
- [ ] Learner transformation defines a clear "Before" and "After."
- [ ] ZERO placeholders like "TBD" remain.
- [ ] Strategic belief is stated as a non-negotiable professional standard.
